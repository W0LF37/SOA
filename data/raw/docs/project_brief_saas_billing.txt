Project Brief: SaaS Subscription Billing Platform

Project Overview:
A billing engine for SaaS products supporting subscription plans, invoicing, and dunning.

Actors:
- Subscriber, Billing Admin, Finance Manager, Developer (API consumer)

Functional Requirements:
1. Subscription plan management: create/edit plans with pricing tiers and trial periods.
2. Payment collection via Stripe with automatic renewal and card retry logic.
3. Invoice generation with PDF download, VAT calculation, and email delivery.
4. Dunning management: automated retry schedule and account suspension on failure.
5. Usage-based billing: metering API for tracking consumption and overage charges.
6. Customer billing portal: view invoices, update payment method, cancel subscription.
7. Revenue reporting: MRR, churn rate, LTV dashboards for finance team.

Non-Functional Requirements:
8. Billing operations must be idempotent to prevent double-charges on retries.
9. All financial transactions must be logged in an immutable audit trail.
10. Stripe webhook processing must complete within 10 seconds to avoid timeout.
