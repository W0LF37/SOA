Project Title:
Online Marketplace Platform

Project Overview:
A web-based marketplace where customers can browse and purchase products from
multiple sellers, manage their orders, and leave product reviews, while sellers
manage their inventory and track sales performance.

Problem Statement:
Small businesses currently sell through social media and manual processes,
resulting in no centralized inventory management, poor order tracking, and
no structured customer feedback mechanism.

Proposed Solution:
Build a unified digital marketplace that connects customers and sellers on a
single platform with secure payments, real-time order tracking, and a
transparent review system.

Target Users:
- Customer
- Seller

Main Features:
- Allow customers to browse and search products by category and keyword
- Allow customers to add products to cart and complete secure checkout
- Allow customers to track their orders in real time after purchase
- Allow sellers to manage product listings, inventory, and pricing
- Allow sellers to view sales analytics and revenue reports
- Allow customers to submit product ratings and written reviews

Expected Benefits:
The platform should handle peak traffic during sale events with response times
under two seconds for all browsing and checkout operations. All payment and
personal data must be encrypted and stored in compliance with data protection
standards. The system should be accessible from both desktop and mobile browsers
without degradation in functionality.

Constraints or Special Notes:
- Must integrate with at least one payment gateway for online transactions
- System must support Arabic and English interfaces
- Ideally support cryptocurrency payments if technically feasible in future phases
- Must handle at least one thousand concurrent users during peak sale periods
