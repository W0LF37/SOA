Project Title:
University Student Portal

Project Overview:
A web-based platform to manage student academic life including course enrollment,
grade tracking, tuition payments, and communication between students, instructors,
advisors, and the registrar office.

Problem Statement:
Students currently rely on multiple disconnected systems and in-person visits to
register courses, track grades, pay tuition, and contact advisors, leading to
delays, missed deadlines, and poor academic visibility.

Proposed Solution:
Build a unified digital portal where students, instructors, academic advisors,
and registrar staff collaborate on a single academic record per student.

Target Users:
- Student
- Instructor
- Academic Advisor
- Registrar

Main Features:
- Register and enroll in available courses for the upcoming semester
- Allow instructors to upload grades and course materials for enrolled students
- Allow academic advisors to review student academic progress and suggest courses
- Generate tuition invoices and process online payment for registered students
- Send automated email notifications to students for deadlines and grade updates
- Allow the registrar to manage course schedules and assign instructors to courses
- Support transcript requests and official document generation for graduating students
- Ideally integrate with a third-party learning management system if budget allows

Expected Benefits:
The system should be highly available with no more than one hour of downtime per
month during the academic year. All student data and payment information must be
encrypted and protected from unauthorized access. The platform should respond
within three seconds for all standard operations under peak load conditions.

Constraints or Special Notes:
- Must support Arabic and English interfaces for all user roles
- System must comply with local university data protection regulations
- Must handle at least five hundred concurrent users during registration periods
- Mobile-friendly interface is preferred but not required for the initial release
