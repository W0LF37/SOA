Project Brief: HR Management System

Project Overview:
A Human Resources platform for managing employees, leave, payroll, and recruitment workflows.

Actors:
- HR Manager, Department Head, Employee, Payroll Officer

Functional Requirements:
1. Employee onboarding and profile management with document upload.
2. Leave request and approval workflow with balance tracking and calendar view.
3. Payroll calculation with tax deductions, allowances, and payslip generation.
4. Attendance tracking via time-in/time-out with overtime calculation.
5. Recruitment module: job postings, applicant tracking, interview scheduling.
6. Performance review workflow with KPI tracking and rating submission.
7. Organizational chart builder with reporting lines and department hierarchy.

Non-Functional Requirements:
8. All payroll data must be encrypted at rest and in transit (AES-256).
9. System must be available 99.9% of the time during business hours.
10. Role-based access: HR Manager sees all data; employees see only their own.
