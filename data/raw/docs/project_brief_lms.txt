Project Brief: Learning Management System (LMS)

Project Overview:
An online education platform for course creation, delivery, student tracking, and certification.

Actors:
- Instructor, Student, Administrator, Content Reviewer

Functional Requirements:
1. Course builder with video lessons, quizzes, assignments, and rich-text content.
2. Student enrollment, progress tracking, and completion certificates generation.
3. Quiz engine with multiple question types, time limits, and auto-grading.
4. Discussion forums per course with moderation and notification features.
5. Live session integration (Zoom/WebRTC) with recording and attendance tracking.
6. Learning path builder: prerequisite courses and adaptive progression rules.
7. Analytics dashboard for instructors: completion rates, quiz scores, engagement.

Non-Functional Requirements:
8. Support concurrent access for at least 5000 simultaneous students.
9. Video streaming must adapt to bandwidth (adaptive bitrate, 360p–1080p).
10. WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility compliance for all student-facing interfaces.
