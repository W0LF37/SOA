Project Brief: Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

Project Overview:
A CRM platform for sales pipeline management, customer tracking, and marketing automation.

Actors:
- Sales Rep, Sales Manager, Marketing Specialist, Customer Support Agent

Functional Requirements:
1. Contact and account management with custom fields and activity timeline.
2. Deal pipeline with drag-and-drop stages, probability weighting, and forecasting.
3. Email campaign builder with templates, scheduling, and open/click tracking.
4. Task and follow-up reminders tied to contacts and deals.
5. Integration with email clients (Gmail/Outlook) for two-way sync.
6. Sales analytics: revenue forecasts, conversion rates, rep performance dashboards.
7. Customer support ticketing linked to CRM contacts with SLA tracking.

Non-Functional Requirements:
8. API response time must not exceed 500ms for all list and detail views.
9. Data import/export for up to 500,000 contact records without timeout.
10. GDPR-compliant data deletion and export on customer request within 72 hours.
